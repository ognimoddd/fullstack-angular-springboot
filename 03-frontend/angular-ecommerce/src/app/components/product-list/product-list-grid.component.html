About Us

It all started a a few summers ago while surfing and skateboarding. Two teenage siblings still in high school became fascinated with fashion by having amazing conversations over delicious drinks. Their primary goal was to create eco-friendly clothing that both skaters and surfers would love. They also wanted to sell their own lemonade, and dreamed of reducing apparel waste and saving money for college, while still attending school.


The founders of the Michael Emma Store decided to sell limited edition non fast fashion clothing and discover methods to recycle clothing. The Michael Emma Store founders are also searching to find ways on how to use or develop technical and process innovation to reduce or eliminate the contribution of clothing to landfill waste and water pollution.

The Michael Emma Store still wants to contribute to saving the bees and to start selling premier grade beeswax products and beverages.
